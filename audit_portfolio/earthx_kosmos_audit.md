Organizations benefit from ambiguity. Without metrics:

- Perpetuation is guaranteed (no performance-based funding)

This is the structural signature of "dialogue theatre" — substantial investment in the **performance** of environmental engagement without commensurate investment in measuring or producing environmental **outcomes**.

Natural systems exhibit emergent transparency (ant pheromone trails communicate clearly). EarthX exhibits **emergent opacity** — information becomes more obscured over time as private foundation structure reduces disclosure and media coverage remains promotional.

EarthX operates in a perfect accountability vacuum:
- No external enforcement (charity raters, watchdogs, donors)

- No market discipline (100% contribution-funded)

This is a **designed accountability vacuum**, not an accidental gap. Every structural choice — private foundation classification, family funding, absence of metrics, fossil fuel sponsorship — reduces accountability pressure.
