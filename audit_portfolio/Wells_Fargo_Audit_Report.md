#### **1. Structural Dissection (7ES Framework)**  
| **Element**       | **Findings**                                                                 | **Weakness Tag** |  
|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------|

| **Output**        | Financial products (loans, accounts), shareholder profits, regulatory fines. | Extractive (profits > customer benefit). |

| **Feedback**      | Customer complaints, regulatory penalties.                                  | Reactive (no systemic adaptation). |  
| **Interface**     | Branches, online platforms, corporate PR.                                   | Opaque (complex fee structures). |  
| **Environment**   | U.S. banking sector, economic inequality.                                   | Exploitative (targets low-income customers). |

- **Processing:** Incentivized fraud via cross-selling quotas.  
- **Controls:** Regulatory capture delayed accountability.
