## Phase 1: Structural Dissection (7ES Framework)

**7ES Audit Note:** Input opacity exceeds 60 percent based on dark money vehicle usage and anonymous donor preferences. This triggers the mandatory worst-case value assumption per MRF audit parameters.

### Element 2: Outputs

**Documented System Products:**

Political outputs include direct candidate support for student government elections, coordination with Trump family members (Donald Trump Jr., Lara Trump), establishment of Turning Point Action 501(c)(4) for direct political advocacy, and early mobilization for JD Vance 2028 presidential campaign with planned operations in Iowa, New Hampshire, and Nevada.
