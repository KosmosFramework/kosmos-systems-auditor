### **Executive Summary**
The TDHCA operates as a classic **hybrid unnatural system**, with structural brittleness, misaligned incentives, and high observer dependency. It scores poorly across FDPs—particularly in **Reciprocal Ethics (RE)**, **Emergent Transparency (ET)**, and **Intellectual Honesty (IH)**—indicating a system that is collapse-prone under stress or loss of public trust. #### **Observer’s Collapse Function (OCF)**
\[
\text{OCF} = \frac{B_R \cdot D_C}{T_S} = \frac{0.75 \times 0.65}{1.2} = 0.41
