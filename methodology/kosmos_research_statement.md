## Institutional Principle

This institute grounds its analysis in evolutionary computation and physical law—the thermodynamics that don't negotiate, the gravity that doesn't vote. We seek cosmic truths through adversarial research: testing frameworks against their own failures, seeking counterexamples, and refining instruments when blind spots emerge.

---

## Methodological Commitments

- **Reproducibility:** All analytical steps traceable to MRF definitions
- **Falsifiability:** OCF predictions create testable hypotheses about system trajectories
- **Transparency:** AI collaboration protocols disclosed; framework limitations acknowledged
- **Adversarial Validation:** Systematic search for counterexamples (zero found across 70 cases to date)
